Hello, my name is Levi Cai, but I usually go by Veevee. I am currently a student at the MIT Media Lab, in the Mediated Matter Group with Prof. Neri Oxman, where I work on swarm systems and robots for large-scale fabrication. Previously I worked in the GRASP Lab with Profs. Dan Lee, Mark Yim, and Lyle Ungar at the Univ. of Pennsylvania on path planning, computer vision, multi-robot systems, modular robotics, and NLP.

I received my BSE in CS and MSE in Robotics from Penn. I also worked at IBM for a year as a software engineer followed by a year as a full-stack software engineer at an education startup, MasterStreet, focused on professional development (now gone).
