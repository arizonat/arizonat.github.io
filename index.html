Hello, my name is Levi Cai, but I usually go by Veevee. I am interested in developing autonomous systems for exploration, especially in the ocean. I am currently a student at the University of Washington in the Aero&Astro department, specializing in controls.

Previously, I have worked on multi-robot systems, construction robots, computer vision, NLP, path planning, and modular robots. I studied with Prof. Neri Oxman at the MIT Media Lab (MS in MAS), and Profs. Dan Lee, Mark Yim, and Lyle Ungar at the GRASP Lab at the University of Pennsylvania (BSE in Computer Science, MSE in Robotics).
